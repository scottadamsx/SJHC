Discover Your Next Trail!

Whether you're a seasoned trekker or just starting out, there's a perfect hike waiting for you in St. John's. Explore different types of adventures!

Easy Strolls & Nature Walks

Perfect for beginners, families, or those looking for a relaxing outing. These trails are generally flat, well-maintained, and offer beautiful scenery with minimal effort.

  • **Typical Distance:** 2-5 km
  • **Elevation:** Minimal
  • **Ideal For:** All ages, leisurely pace, photography

Moderate Day Hikes

A step up in challenge, these hikes often involve some inclines, uneven terrain, and longer distances. They're great for building stamina and exploring more diverse landscapes.

  • **Typical Distance:** 5-10 km
  • **Elevation:** Moderate climbs
  • **Ideal For:** Regular walkers, fitness enthusiasts

Challenging Treks & Half-Day Adventures

For experienced hikers seeking a more rigorous workout. Expect significant elevation changes, rugged paths, and longer durations, rewarding you with breathtaking views.

  • **Typical Distance:** 10+ km
  • **Elevation:** Steep, sustained climbs
  • **Ideal For:** Experienced hikers, endurance training

Themed & Special Hikes

Join us for unique events like scavenger hunts, birdwatching tours, or historical walks. These hikes combine outdoor activity with engaging themes and community fun.

  • **Focus:** Community, education, unique experiences
  • **Variations:** Family-friendly, educational, seasonal
  • **Ideal For:** Socializers, those seeking something different
