About St. John's Hike Club

Our Mission

St. John’s Hike Club was established to bring together outdoor enthusiasts who enjoy exploring the natural beauty of Newfoundland. Our mission is to provide organized, enjoyable, and safe hiking experiences for people of all skill levels.

History

Founded on January 26, 2025, by a group of passionate hikers, St. John’s Hike Club has quickly become a key community resource for outdoor activities in St. John's. We offer regular hikes on the East Coast Trail and other nearby paths, fostering a supportive and inclusive community atmosphere.

Activities and Programs

We organize hikes every second Sunday, provide essential hiking tips, and collaborate with local businesses like The Outfitters for gear and East Coast Trail Association for trail maintenance and events. Our activities are designed to promote physical health, environmental awareness, and community engagement.
