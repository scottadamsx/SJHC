Welcome Hikers!

St. John’s Hike Club was founded to connect hiking enthusiasts in the St. John's area, providing organized group hikes along the beautiful East Coast Trail and other local paths. Whether you're looking to explore breathtaking views, improve your physical health, or meet new people with similar interests, our club offers something for everyone.

Why Join Our Club?

Beyond the trails, St. John’s Hike Club is a community. We host regular meetings, planning sessions, and social events to foster a sense of community and shared adventure among our members. Membership is free, and all are welcome!

About Us

We are dedicated to promoting healthy lifestyles through hiking and to providing an inclusive, welcoming environment for all community members to explore the natural beauty of Newfoundland.
